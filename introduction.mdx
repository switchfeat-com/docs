## Key concepts

SwitchFeat is based on three simple concepts: Flags, Segments, Rules. Once you master those, managing your feature flags
is going to be very easy.

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card
    title="Flags"
    icon="pen-to-square"
    href="/concepts/flags"
  >
    Flags are usually features that you want to switch on/off in your application. 
  </Card>
  <Card
    title="Segments"
    icon="image"
    href="/concepts/segments"
  >
    Segments group multiple users together to share feature visibility or other traits.
  </Card>
   <Card
    title="Rules"
    icon="image"
    href="/concepts/rules"
  >
    Rules are a set of segments which could be assigned to one or more flags.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>
